About 42nd & Empire

It all started with a single phrase from my husband's work "support hero."

For years I'd dabbled in sewing, and most around the time I heard about the support hero position at New Relic, I had made a fun, sparkly cape for my daughter. So I asked my husband if he thought maybe the support hero at work should have a cape. He loved the idea, so I whipped out an adult length cape with a hand-stitched Ruby logo that you can see on the home page.

It was a huge hit. The cape was frequently worn around the office, and my husband even gave a talk at his local users group, and then at RailsConf 2014, wearing the majestic Ruby cape.

The cape was so popular in fact that people kept asking here they could get them. 42nd & Empire is the result of that question. I'm here for all your nerdy custom sewing needs, but our hearts are in capes and things that make the workplace just a little more... sparkly.
