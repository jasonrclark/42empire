The Cape Itself

  • Capes are custom, so your design is yours
  • You can be as involved in the design process as you want! Anywhere from "Just make something cool," to getting in the thick of material and color choices.
  • Typical design includes:
    • Colored satin lining
    • Sparkly exterior fabric
    • Hand-embroidered felt badge
    • Soft cotton webbing strap
    • Reflective snap buckle
